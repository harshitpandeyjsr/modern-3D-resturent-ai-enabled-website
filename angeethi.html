The Philosophy

Built around a fire that never goes out

Angeethi opened in 2019 with one rule: if it can't be cooked over live coal, it doesn't go on the menu. No induction, no microwaves, no shortcuts hiding behind a pass.

Our grain, our dairy, and most of our produce travel less than 200km to reach the kitchen. We work directly with three farming families outside Mumbai who grow specifically for our fire — slower vegetables, denser grains, things that can survive heat without disappearing.

What you taste on the plate is mostly restraint. Good coal does the rest.
